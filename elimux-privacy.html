1. Information We Collect

We may collect: information you provide when searching for courses (such as your country, qualification level, and study preferences); contact details if you submit an enquiry or sign up; and basic technical information such as your device type and general location, collected automatically to help the service work.

We use your information to: provide course recommendations and search results; connect you with institutions or agents when you request it; improve our platform; and communicate with you about your enquiries. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Sharing With Institutions

When you choose to send an enquiry or application to a university, college, or agent through Elimux, the relevant details you provide are shared with that institution so they can respond to you. We share only what is necessary for that purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. Sensitive credentials are never stored in plain text. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Elimux uses minimal local storage to remember your preferences (such as your selected country). We do not use invasive tracking.
