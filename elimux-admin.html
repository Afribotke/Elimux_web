About Elimux

Elimux helps students discover the right course, anywhere — in plain language, matched to their qualifications, budget, and goals. We bring universities, colleges, TVETs, and verified agents into one place so students can compare genuine, accredited options and connect directly with the institutions that offer them.

Why we exist

Too many students miss good opportunities simply because they never hear about them — or can't tell which courses and providers are genuinely recognised. Elimux exists to close that gap, starting in Kenya and growing across Africa and beyond.

How it works

Tell Elimux what you want to study, where, and your budget. Our AI search returns your best matches from our directory of institutions — drawn directly from verified sources, with a "Source Verified" badge so you know the information comes from the institution itself.

Who we are

Elimux is built by Afribot Ventures Limited, based in Nairobi, Kenya, with a Kenya-first focus and an Africa-wide and global outlook.
